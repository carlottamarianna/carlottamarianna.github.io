About Me

I grew up in Italy, where I earned a BA in Philosophy. I then pursued a Master's degree in Cognitive Science and Linguistics at the École Normale Supérieure in Paris, completing it in 2026. My research there explored different facets of communicative efficiency, with a particular focus on the roles of utterance length and vagueness. In 2025, I also worked as a research collaborator at Princeton University.
